About Dr. S. Chowdhury

Dr. Chowdhury is an internationally-accredited cardiologist & electrophysiologist with years of training & experience dedicated to providing exceptional cardiac care. With a compassionate approach and a focus on evidence-based medicine, Dr. Chowdhury specialises in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of various heart disease. His extensive expertise is in managing arrhythmias including atrial fibrillation, flutter, supraventricular tachycardais, ventricular arrhythmias and providing cardiac devices care. Dr. Chowdhury is committed to empowering patients with knowledge about their heart health and guiding them towards a healthier, more active life.

Dr. Chowdhury completed his training in the north-west UK, followed by electrophysiology fellowship in Canada. He is a triple-accredited electrophysiologist & active member of various British, European and American cardiac professionals societies and regularly contribute to medical research and health initiatives.
